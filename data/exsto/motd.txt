    <h3="">To change the MOTD:
<ol>
<li>Open up the quick menu, using either !menu, +ExQuick, or +ExQuickToggle.<br></li>
<li>Click on the pages icon (second in from the left on the top) and then navigate to the Settings page.<br></li>
<li>Click on the MOTD category.  You should see a Location setting.<br></li>
<li>You can change the location setting to either be an HTTP link, or a location in the data directory.  Currently, it is set to exsto/motd.txt.  This means it will look in data/exsto/motd.txt for your MOTD.<br></li>
<li>
  Feel free to remove the contents of exsto/motd.txt and place your own MOTD, or put it somewhere else in the data directory and point the location setting there.</li></ol>
